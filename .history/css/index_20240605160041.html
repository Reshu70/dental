ABOUT US

Welcome to Ashishdentalclinic

Established in 2014, Ashish Dental Clinic has been serving the Ludhiana community with top-notch dental care for over 10 years. Our clinic was founded by Dr. Ashish Singla, a highly skilled and compassionate dentist with a passion for improving oral health and enhancing smiles. Dr. Ashish’s vision was to create a welcoming and patient-centered dental practice that prioritizes the comfort and well-being of every individual who walks through our doors.

Our Mission

At Ashish Dental Clinic, our mission is simple yet profound: to provide the highest quality dental care in a warm, friendly, and comfortable environment. We are dedicated to helping our patients achieve and maintain healthy, beautiful smiles that they can confidently share with the world.

Our Approach

What sets us apart is our patient-centric approach. We understand that visiting the dentist can be a source of anxiety for some, so we've designed our clinic to be a stress-free and soothing place. From our friendly staff to our modern, state-of-the-art equipment, we ensure that your experience with us is as pleasant as possible..
