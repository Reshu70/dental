About Ashish Dental Clinic Ludhiana

Established in 2014, Ashish Dental Clinic has been serving the Ludhiana community with top-notch dental care for over 10 years. Our clinic was founded by Dr. Ashish Singla, a highly skilled and compassionate dentist with a passion for improving oral health and enhancing smiles.

General Dentistry

From routine check-ups and cleanings to fillings and extractions, we are here to take care of all your general dental needs.

Restorative Dentistry

If you’re dealing with damaged or missing teeth, our restorative treatments such as crowns, bridges, and dental implants can help restore your smile’s function and appearance.

Orthodontics: We offer orthodontic solutions like braces and Invisalign to align your teeth and correct bite issues.

Emergency Dentistry: Dental emergencies can happen at any time. We provide prompt and compassionate care when you need it most.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We believe that every patient deserves the highest level of care and attention. Our team is dedicated to ongoing education and training to ensure that we can provide you with the latest advancements in dental care. Your safety and well-being are paramount to us, and we adhere to the strictest sterilization and hygiene protocols
